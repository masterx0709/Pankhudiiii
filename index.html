How to Use

Simple Daily Routine

Take Pankhudi twice daily after meals — morning and evening — for best results.

1
Morning — After Breakfast
Take 20–30 ml of Pankhudi Juice after your morning meal. Mix in water if preferred.
2
Evening — After Dinner
Take another 20–30 ml dose after your evening meal for complete daily nourishment.
3
Shake Well Before Use
Always shake the bottle well before pouring — ensures all natural herbal ingredients are evenly mixed.
4
Store in Cool, Dry Place
Keep away from direct sunlight. Refrigerate after opening for best shelf life and freshness.
